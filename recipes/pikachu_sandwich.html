Pikachu Sandwich

Description

What could be better than a freshly made sandwich? How about a freshly caught pikachu eating said sandwich? Lets two-birds-one-stone this recipe so you can can see your new friend in sandwich-enjoying bliss!

Ingredients

  • Pokeballs
  • Tall grass
  • A starter pokemon (and you had better have chosen charmander)
  • A sandwich
  1. Walk through grass until wild pikachu appears
  2. Call out your starter pokemon
  3. Use damage inflicting attacks to lower pikachu's health
  4. Throw a pokeball at the wild pikachu
  5. You have captured a wild pikachu! Now call it out a give it a well deserved sandwich
  6. Chu! ~~
